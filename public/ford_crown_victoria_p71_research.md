# Ford Crown Victoria Police Interceptor (2005-2011) Research

## 2005 Model Year

### Engine
*   **Type:** 4.6L SOHC V8
*   **Displacement:** 4601 cc (281 cu in)
*   **Horsepower:** 250 hp @ 5000 rpm
*   **Torque:** 297 lb-ft @ 4000 rpm
*   **Fuel Type:** Gasoline
*   **Transmission:** 4-speed automatic (4R70W/4R75W)
*   **Police Enhancements:** Heavy-duty cooling system, Larger alternator (200A)
*   **Common Issues:** Intake manifold cracking, Spark plug ejection, Transmission issues (reverse, overdrive)

### Chassis & Suspension
*   **Frame Type:** Full perimeter frame
*   **Front Suspension:** Independent, short/long arm (SLA) with coil springs, heavy-duty shocks, stabilizer bar
*   **Rear Suspension:** Solid axle, 4-link with coil springs, heavy-duty shocks, stabilizer bar
*   **Braking System:** 4-wheel disc with ABS, 11.65-inch front rotors, 11.2-inch rear rotors
*   **Steering System:** Power-assisted recirculating ball
*   **Wheels & Tires:** 16x7-inch steel wheels, P235/55R16 Goodyear Eagle RS-A tires
*   **Police Reinforcements:** Reinforced frame sections, Heavy-duty control arms

### Body & Exterior
*   **Overall dimensions:** 212" L x 78.2" W x 58.3" H
*   **Wheelbase:** 114.7"
*   **Curb weight:** ~4,057 lbs
*   **Body construction details:** Body-on-frame
*   **Police-specific exterior features:** Spotlight provisions, push bar mounts, antenna mounts
*   **Common rust areas or structural weaknesses:** Rear frame sections, rocker panels

### Interior
*   **Seating configuration and materials:** Heavy-duty cloth front seats, vinyl rear seat
*   **Dashboard layout and instrumentation:** Standard Crown Victoria layout with police-specific calibration
*   **Police-specific interior provisions:** Center console delete, auxiliary wiring, computer mounts, prisoner cage mounts
*   **HVAC system details:** Standard Ford HVAC system

### Electrical System
*   **Battery specifications:** 78 Ah
*   **Alternator output:** 200A
*   **Wiring harness details for police equipment:** Separate wiring harness for police equipment
*   **Auxiliary power points and fuse box configurations:** Additional power points and fuse box for police equipment

### Performance
*   **0-60 mph acceleration times:** ~8.5 seconds
*   **Top speed:** ~130 mph (governed)
*   **Braking distance:** ~140 ft from 60 mph
*   **Handling characteristics and police-tuned suspension impact:** Heavy-duty suspension provides improved handling and stability

### Safety Features
*   **Airbag system details:** Driver and passenger front airbags
*   **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS):** Standard
*   **Traction Control System (TCS) availability:** Optional
*   **Crash test ratings:** 5-star frontal crash rating (NHTSA)

### Production & Model Year Differences
*   **Key changes or updates between the 2005 and 2011 model years for the P71 package:** See subsequent model years for details.
*   **Distinguishing features from civilian Crown Victoria models:** Heavy-duty components, police-specific features, "Police Interceptor" badging
